About Us

Pizzeria Bellissima has been in business since 2017 in Kenya and Rwanda. All of our pizzas are made according to the Pizzare family tradition. We use only the highest-quality ingredients, including buffalo-milk mozzarella and San Marzano tomatoes imported from Italy as well as our fresh, home-made mozzarella.
All pizzas are drizzled with 100% Italian extra-virgin olive oil and then baked to perfection at 850 degrees in a Pizzare Bellissima wood-fired brick oven imported from Italy.
